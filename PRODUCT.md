# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Startup founders and entrepreneurs — the "idea guy" archetype. They arrive with a raw business concept and a need to make it clear, structured, and grounded. Their emotional state is a mix of excitement and uncertainty. They need the tool to do the heavy lifting of organizing their thoughts so they can focus on decisions, not formatting.

## Product Purpose

RocketMap is an AI-powered Business Model Canvas validation tool. It helps founders stress-test startup ideas through:

- Interactive 9-block Business Model Canvas
- AI chat assistance for filling blocks and generating insights
- Market research tools (TAM/SAM/SOM, competitive landscape, personas, segmentation)
- Segment evaluation (decision matrices, beachhead analysis, radar charts)
- Unit economics analysis and sensitivity modeling

Success means a founder enters with an idea and leaves with a validated, structured plan — knowing exactly what to do next.

## Brand Personality

**Smart planner. The papyrus for business.**

- Timeless craft meets modern intelligence
- Grounding, not hyping
- Ancient solidity with AI speed
- The medium is the message: the interface exists to capture and refine thought, not to entertain

## Anti-references

- No corporate blue dashboards (McKinsey, Salesforce aesthetics)
- No Notion-clone minimalism (endless white space, ghostly typography)
- No gamified/confetti UI (celebration animations, progress badges, dopamine loops)
- No SaaS hero-metric templates (big numbers, gradient accents, stats grids)
- No identical card-grid feature lists (icon + heading + text, repeated endlessly)

## Design Principles

1. **Ground the dreamer** — every interaction makes an abstract idea more concrete, not more exciting. The tool reduces uncertainty, not amplifies hype.
2. **Timeless craft, modern intelligence** — the gravity of business planning combined with the speed of AI assistance. Ancient papyrus, silicon brain.
3. **Clarity over density** — show what's needed for the current decision, hide what isn't. No feature parade, no sidebar overload.
4. **The papyrus principle** — the interface is a medium for thought, not a destination. It should feel like writing on something that matters.
5. **Leave with direction** — every session ends with a clear next step, not just more data. The output is actionable, not merely documented.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance target
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` for all animations and transitions
- Ensure sufficient color contrast for data visualizations (charts, scorecards)
- Keyboard-navigable canvas blocks and AI chat interface
